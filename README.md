# Valheim AI Bot RL Trainer

Training AI model to play Valheim with reinforcement learning.

## Info

**valheim_ai_v1.py**
- Basic training with limited
- Random actions:
  - move forward (w)
  - move backward (s)
  - move left (a)
  - move right (d)
  - attack (mouse left)
  - jump (space)
  - interact (e)

**valheim_ai_v2.py**
- Improved training
- Additional random actions:
  - look left
  - look right
  - look down
  - look up
- Additional rewards/penalties
- Untested

**valheim_ai_v2.10.py**
- Additional debugging
- Untested

**valheim_ai_v2.25.py**
- Improved training
- Additional actions
- Additional rewards/penalties
- HUD tracking
